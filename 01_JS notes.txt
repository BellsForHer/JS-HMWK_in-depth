JS vs. Ecmascript

JS created in 1995

Since 1997 JS relies on Ecmascript

JS relies on the regulations and guidelines of ecmasctipt. Blueprint for scripting langues like JS. 

ES5 2009 first major release of Ecmascript

ES6 2015 latest version, includes implementations of let and const keywords, keywords, promoising, for loops

ES upgrades each year but takes time to implement.
